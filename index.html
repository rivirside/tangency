Our Mission

To serve as a crucial point of tangency between innovative ideas, grassroots movements, and vital resources. We are dedicated to fostering a more interconnected and effective global activist community by advancing activism literacy and empowering changemakers to achieve a just, equitable, and sustainable future.

What is Activism Literacy?

Activism Literacy is the essential framework of knowledge, skills, and critical understanding required to engage in effective, sustainable, and impactful social change. It moves beyond the initial passion that sparks a movement, providing the crucial tools to analyze power structures, build strategic campaigns, and foster resilient communities. While passion provides the fuel, literacy is the engine that drives movements forward.

Featured Micro-Grant: Food Systems Coalition (FSC)

Project: North Central Florida Food System Public Resource Hub

Our foundation awarded an $880 micro-grant to this grassroots organization to enhance their mission of improving local food system accessibility and justice. This project addresses a critical need for a centralized, public data repository to help community stakeholders make informed decisions. Our funding directly supported a 3-year domain registration, web hosting, and compensation for community website reviewers, turning a vital idea into a tangible resource.
