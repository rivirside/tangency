Policy One-Pager Studio

Translate complex legislation into a concise explainer that organizers, volunteers, and officials can understand in five minutes.

Focus: Policy Education Format: Studio Session Time: 75 minutes Team: Writers + policy leads

Perfect for Lobby days, canvasses, legislative visits, partner briefings.

Deliverable Designed one-pager (PDF + print) with CTA + QR code.

Materials Bill text, fact sheets, design template, quote bank.

Outputs Shared doc you can localize by swapping stats.

Prep checklist

  • Gather the bill summary, fiscal note, and any opposition talking points.
  • Interview a directly impacted person or frontline partner for a quote.
  • Choose the template (portrait or landscape) and pre-load your brand colors.

Studio flow

  1. Outline (15 min). Write the human-centered lead: problem, people affected, proposed fix.
  2. Break it down (20 min). Create three sections: What the bill does, how it’s paid for, what happens if it fails. Use bullets and icons.
  3. Add story + CTA (15 min). Insert a quote or mini case study plus a QR code linking to deeper resources or action forms.
  4. Peer review (15 min). Policy lead checks accuracy; organizer ensures clarity for everyday readers.
  5. Publish (10 min). Export to PDF for print + PNG for digital sharing.

Tips

  • Use plain language—swap “tranche” for “dollars in the second year,” etc.
  • Leave a stats slot that can be localized for different districts.
  • Embed a shortened link so people without smartphones can still follow up.
