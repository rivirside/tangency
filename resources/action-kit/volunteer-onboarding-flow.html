Action Kit

Volunteer Onboarding Flow

Convert sign-ups into active teammates within five days using automated emails, orientation blocks, and buddy systems.

Focus: Organizing Operations Format: Workflow Time: Build once, automate Team: Operations + team leads

Perfect for Rapid scaling periods, fellowship launches, distributed chapters.

Deliverable Automated journey map + task list.

Materials Email platform, calendar tool, intake survey, buddy roster.

Outputs Conversion metrics + feedback loop.

Flow overview

The flow consists of three touch points: instant welcome email, orientation session, and first assignment with a buddy check-in. Track how many people move through each stage so you can improve scripts.

Prep checklist

  • Write a welcome email that names two specific roles people can try immediately.
  • Create a Typeform or Google Form to capture skills, schedule, and languages.
  • Block recurring 30-minute orientation sessions (virtual or hybrid).
  • Recruit a buddy bench—experienced volunteers willing to shadow new folks for two weeks.

Implementation steps

  1. Day 0 – Email. Auto-send the welcome note with the intake survey plus a Calendly link to orientation.
  2. Day 2 – Orientation. Review mission, campaigns, culture norms, and end with a menu of starter tasks. Get each attendee to pick one on the spot.
  3. Day 3 – Assignment. Team leads receive the intake responses + task choice and send a direct Slack/WhatsApp intro with their buddy.
  4. Day 5 – Buddy check-in. Buddies confirm progress, answer questions, and log any blockers in the onboarding tracker.

Tips

  • Measure conversion by dividing the number of people who take a first task by total sign-ups.
  • Record a 5-minute orientation video for people who can’t attend live and pair it with a quiz to confirm completion.
  • Celebrate Day 5 wins publicly to reinforce the culture of action.

How to use this kit

  1. Draft the journey map. Document every touchpoint from sign-up to first task and confirm owners.
  2. Automate the welcome email + intake form. Make sure new folks get a response within minutes.
  3. Host weekly orientation blocks. End each session by having attendees choose their starter assignment.
  4. Pair them with a buddy and log progress. Buddies run the Day 5 check-in and report conversions during staff meetings.
