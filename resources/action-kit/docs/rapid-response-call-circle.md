# Rapid Response Call Circle

_A Tangency Action Kit_

## Snapshot
- **Focus:** Mobilization
- **Format:** Checklist
- **Time:** Prep once, deploy anytime
- **Ideal team:** 10–200 volunteers
- **Great for:** Court rulings, urgent rallies, fundraising crises, policy alerts
- **Deliverable:** Updated call tree spreadsheet + conversation log
- **Materials:** Shared sheet, scripts, disposition codes, texting backup

## Prep checklist
1. Segment supporters into pods of 8–10 based on geography or language.
2. Assign a captain and backup captain per pod.
3. Write two scripts: informational briefing + mobilization ask.
4. Build a shared log (Airtable/Sheet) with columns for status, notes, follow-ups.

## Deployment
1. **Trigger the tree.** Lead organizer briefs captains via Zoom or voice memo; captains call their pod within 15 minutes.
2. **Track responses.** Captains log Ready / Need info / Can’t help outcomes in the shared sheet.
3. **Escalate gaps.** After two failed attempts, backups or staff text/WhatsApp the contact.
4. **Close the loop.** Captains report totals and intel; send thank-you SMS plus next steps to everyone who answered.

## Tips
- Provide voice-note examples to keep tone grounded.
- Rotate captains quarterly to prevent burnout and grow leaders.
- Export logs after each activation to analyze who consistently responds.

## Tools & templates
- Call tree roster (Google Sheet)
- Rapid response scripts (briefing + mobilization)
- Debrief form for intel and resource requests
