# Policy One-Pager Template

**Header:** Bill name + one-line purpose.

## Problem
- Two sentences describing who is harmed and how.

## Solution
- Bullet list describing what the bill does.

## Funding / Implementation
- Note how program is paid for and the agency responsible.

## Consequences if it fails
- Two bullets describing status quo.

## Take Action
- Call/Email target info + QR code placeholder.

## Story/Quote
> "[Impacted person quote]."

## Contact
Campaign lead + email/phone.
