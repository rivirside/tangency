Press Day Toolkit

Plan a 24-hour earned media blitz that surrounds your report release or action with aligned spokespeople, quotes, and visuals.

Focus: Media Outreach Format: Template Pack Time: Half-day build Team: Comms crew + spokespeople

Perfect for Report launches, executive actions, rapid response to investigations.

Deliverable Media advisory, press list, b-roll folder, quote bank.

Materials Shared drive, spokesperson bios, embargo plan.

Outputs Coverage tracker + social proof plan.

Prep checklist

  • Draft a two-paragraph media advisory with headline, hook, and spokespeople.
  • Package assets (photos, logos, b-roll) in one folder with filenames that make sense.
  • Identify three validators (partners, academics, workers) who can offer quotes.
  • Decide which outlets get embargoed previews and schedule those briefings.

Press day timeline

  1. T-24 hours. Send embargoed advisory to key reporters with offer for one-on-one background call.
  2. T-3 hours. Share the public advisory + asset folder with your full press list. Post teaser graphics on social.
  3. Go live. Release the report/action, blast updates on every channel, and pitch short follow-up angles tailored to each outlet.
  4. Post-release. Track coverage, post clips to your website, and send thank-yous plus “what’s next” to reporters and validators.

Tips

  • Assign one person to run the war room inbox so reporters get rapid responses.
  • Prep social media proof (ally quotes, infographics) to drop the moment the first story lands.
  • Update your media tracker with open rates and replies so list hygiene stays tight.
