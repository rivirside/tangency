Healing Circle Routine

Hold a 30–45 minute circle that lets teams debrief wins and losses, share care requests, and leave with renewed clarity.

Focus: Wellbeing Format: Facilitation Guide Time: 30–45 minutes Team: Any size

Perfect for Post-action debriefs, campaign pivots, staff meetings after crises.

Deliverable Shared agreements, anonymized themes, support commitments.

Materials Timer, talking piece, notes doc, optional virtual whiteboard.

Outputs List of pauses/delegations/resourcing decisions.

Prep checklist

  • Choose a facilitator and a note-taker (can rotate).
  • Prepare opening grounding (breath, stretch, music, or visualization).
  • Set up a shared document to capture anonymized themes and follow-ups.

Circle flow

  1. Welcome + agreements (5 min). Review confidentiality, step up/step back, and care options.
  2. Grounding (5 min). Guide a short breathing or somatic practice.
  3. Shares (20–25 min). Each person offers one high, one low, and one request for support using a talking piece or virtual queue.
  4. Collective decisions (10 min). Identify what the team will pause, delegate, or resource based on the shares.
  5. Close (5 min). Appreciations round + reminder of support options (therapy funds, rest days, etc.).

Tips

  • Rotate facilitators so care work is shared.
  • Offer multiple ways to participate (chat, shared doc) for folks who don’t want to speak aloud.
  • Summarize anonymized themes and share them with leadership so structural fixes follow.
