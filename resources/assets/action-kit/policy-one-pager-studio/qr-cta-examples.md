# QR CTA Examples

1. **Email your representative** – Link QR to pre-written email form.
2. **Pledge to testify** – QR opens short sign-up form.
3. **Read the full bill** – QR links to legislative text for deeper dives.
4. **Donate to field program** – QR to secure giving page.
5. **Join text list** – QR to SMS opt-in (include disclosure).
