Amplify Your Impact

As a student-organized, grassroots 501(c)(3), the Tangency Foundation offers a unique partnership opportunity. Empower your employees to make a lasting difference by connecting with a cause that is authentic, agile, and deeply committed to community-level change.

Turn Your Passion into Matched Donations

The Tangency Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization on corporate giving platforms like Benevity, which serves companies like Google, Starbucks, and many more. This makes it easy for employees to amplify their impact. When you donate or volunteer with us and log your activity through your employer's portal, your company provides a matching financial contribution, doubling your support for our mission.
