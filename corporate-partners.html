Amplify Your Impact

Tangency Foundation is a verified Benevity partner (Cause ID 840-852519445). We’ve already collaborated with teams at companies like Starbucks and Google, logging volunteer hours and matched donations that fuel scholarships, micro-grants, and literacy projects. If your employer offers matching, we’re ready to help you turn those benefits into real community wins.

How Matching Works

Volunteer Match

Log your volunteer time with Tangency in Benevity and your employer can convert those hours into a monetary donation for our programs. We provide sign-in sheets, project briefs, and post-event impact notes so it’s simple to submit.

Financial Match

Make a one-time or recurring donation through your employer portal and request the match directly to Tangency Foundation. Every dollar you give goes further when your company mirrors it.

Custom Projects & ERG Partnerships

Planning a day of service, hackathon, or cause-focused campaign? We’ll co-design an experience that aligns with your CSR goals and our mission. Employees get to lead meaningful work, and matching funds sustain the initiatives they build.

Ready to start? Search “Tangency Foundation” on Benevity, then email contact@activisthq.org so we can support your submission.

What We Do With Your Support

We provide scholarships to emerging organizers, micro-grants to grassroots campaigns, and custom trainings rooted in Activism Literacy. Matching dollars and logged volunteer hours keep those offerings free for communities that need them most.

Every partner brings unique skills—designers have helped us create civic education zines, engineers have prototyped digital safety tools, and policy folks have mentored youth advocates drafting testimony.

Project Ideas You Can Lead

Bring your creativity. We’ll handle the scaffolding, compliance, and reporting so your Benevity match goes through smoothly.

Community Fundraiser

Organize a workplace drive to support a local mutual aid group or pet shelter, then request a corporate match to double the impact.

Civic Education Studio

Design voter education toolkits, zines, or workshop decks that we can use in schools and community centers across our network.

Action & Advocacy Days

Help us plan a sit-in, teach-in, or awareness event that blends educational content with clear advocacy actions.

Storytelling & Media

Create a social media mini-series, podcast segment, or awareness campaign that spotlights an issue area and directs people to tangible next steps.

Campaign Lab

Facilitate a short sprint where employees prototype a social awareness campaign or digital resource; we’ll guide the framework and publish the finished tool.

Skills-Based Mentorship

Pair up with a youth fellow or grassroots partner to co-build budgets, messaging, or research, logging every hour for match eligibility.

Getting Started Checklist

  1. Search “Tangency Foundation” on your Benevity or corporate giving portal.
  2. Select whether you’re logging volunteer hours or requesting a donation match.
  3. Email contact@activisthq.org with your project idea or volunteer interest.
  4. We’ll confirm details, supply any required documentation, and send you an impact recap for your internal reporting.
